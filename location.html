LOCATION

Gracefully situated on Grønningen 5, 1st floor – in the very heart of Copenhagen – this distinguished residence offers sweeping, uninterrupted views across the historic grounds of Kastellet and its surrounding parkland. From the moment you step inside, the home invites a sense of calm, elevated by its rare combination of grand proportions and understated elegance. Just beyond the doorstep, you’ll find yourself surrounded by some of the city’s finest offerings: Michelin-starred restaurants, curated designer boutiques, and world-class cultural institutions – all woven into the charm of one of Copenhagen’s most sought-after neighborhoods. This is city living at its most cultivated, where history, nature, and modern refinement meet in perfect harmony.
